## üß± Week 1‚Äì2: Foundation (Core Website Fixes)

### 1) Create 3 Essential Service Pages with AI-Optimized Structure

Don't build 50 pages‚Äîbuild 3 exceptional ones:

- **Custom Audio Cables** (product page)
- **Patchbay Design Consultation** (service page)
- **Case Studies/Portfolio** (trust page)

**For each page:**

- **Lead with a 50-word direct answer** to the main question someone would ask
- **Use question-based H2 headings** ("What cable types do you offer?", "How long does custom fabrication take?")
- **Add 2-3 customer testimonials** with names and studio/company
- **Include clear pricing framework** or starting prices
- **One strong CTA** per page (Request Quote, Book Consultation)

**Time:** 6-8 focused hours total

**ROI:** Converts existing traffic + AI visibility + trust building

---

### 2) Implement Basic Schema Markup

Add **FAQ schema** to your 3 core pages. This is the highest-ROI structured data for small sites.

Use Google's free Schema Markup Generator or a WordPress plugin like Rank Math.

**Time:** 1-2 hours

**ROI:** Dramatically improves AI visibility + helps Google understand your content

---

### 3) Set Up Google Business Profile (If Applicable)

If you serve a local/regional area or have a physical location:

- Complete every field
- Add 10+ high-quality photos of your work
- Request 5-10 reviews from past customers

**Time:** 2-3 hours

**ROI:** Immediate local search visibility + costs $0

## ‚úçÔ∏è Week 3‚Äì4: Content That Does Double Duty

### 4) Write 2‚Äì3 ‚ÄúPillar Articles‚Äù That Answer Common Questions

Focus on questions your ideal customers actually ask. Based on your business:

- "How to Choose the Right Cable Gauge for Studio Applications"
- "Balanced vs Unbalanced Audio Cables: When to Use Each"
- "Patchbay Design 101: Planning Your Studio Signal Flow"

**Format for humans AND AI engines:**

- Start with a 60-word summary answer
- Use question-based H2s
- Keep paragraphs to 2-3 lines
- Add FAQ schema
- Include real examples from your projects

**Time:** 3-4 hours per article

**ROI:** Works as:

- Organic SEO traffic generator
- Lead magnet (offer as PDF downloads)
- Email nurture content
- AI citation source
- LinkedIn post material

---

### 5) Repurpose Each Article Into 3‚Äì5 Formats

Don't create more‚Äîrepurpose strategically:

- Break each article into **5-7 LinkedIn posts**
- Record a **10-minute talking-head video** explaining the concept (phone camera is fine)
- Create **3-5 Instagram carousel posts** with key insights
- Extract **quotes/tips for social media** throughout the month

One 2,000-word article becomes 20+ pieces of content across platforms.

**Time:** 30 minutes to repurpose

**ROI:** Builds cross-platform authority that AI engines value

## ü§ñ Month 3: Automation & Systems

### 8) Set Up a Simple Email Sequence

Use a free tier of email software (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or Brevo free plans).

**Lead magnet:** "Studio Cabling Specification Guide" (PDF version of your pillar article)

**5-email welcome sequence:**

1. Deliver the guide + introduce yourself
2. Case study example (studio transformation)
3. Common cabling mistakes (educational)
4. How your consultation process works
5. Consultation booking offer with testimonial

**Time:** One focused day to set up

**ROI:** Runs forever automatically. Email marketing delivers $42 ROI per $1 spent

---

### 9) Install Live Chat (AI-Powered)

Use a free AI chatbot like Tidio or Chatbase trained on your FAQ content:

- Answers technical questions 24/7
- Captures leads when you're unavailable
- Takes 2 hours to set up properly

**Time:** 2 hours

**ROI:** Prevents "I'll contact them later" (which never happens)
